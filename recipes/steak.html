Steak Recipe

Make the Juciest Steak

This salt and pepper rib eye steak recipe, made with bone-in-rib eye, salt, and pepper either on the grill or stovetop, provides a foolproof technique for perfectly cooked steak.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Flakes
  • Dried Parsley

Steps

  1. Let meat defrost to room temperature
  2. Sprinkle salt on both sides of the steak. Remember to add salt to the sides of the steak.
  3. Sprinle pepper to the on both sides of the steak.
  4. Lightly add garlic flakes to both sides of the steak.
  5. Lightly add dried parsely to both sides of the steak.
  6. Grill the steak to your desired internal temperature.
  7. Add butter to the top of the steak and allow to rest for a minimum of 5 minutes.
  8. Enjoy
